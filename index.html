About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity Bootcamp 2025 Q2 is an 8-week hands-on programme designed to empower professionals with practical AI skills. Through this course, I am learning to:

  • Create professional websites, documents, and presentations using AI
  • Perform literature reviews, analyze data, and build dashboards with intuitive AI tools
  • Design custom chatbots for domain-specific queries
  • Set up automated workflows
  • Utilize AI for creating and editing multimedia content
  • Develop practical AI solutions integrated into daily projects

By the end of this course, I aim to harness AI to save time, improve quality, and solve real-world challenges across research and public health programming.
